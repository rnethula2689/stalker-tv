Stalker TV

A lightweight IPTV player for Android & Fire TV — live TV, movies & series, search, subtitles.

⬇ Download the app

Direct link: is.gd/stalkertvfiretv

Fire TV / Android TV

  1. Install the free Downloader app from the Appstore.
  2. Settings → My Fire TV → Developer Options → enable Install unknown apps for Downloader.
  3. Open Downloader, enter is.gd/stalkertvfiretv, then Download → Install → Open.

Android phone / tablet

  1. Open is.gd/stalkertvfiretv in your browser to download the APK.
  2. Tap it and allow install from unknown sources when prompted.

First-time setup

  • Open ⚙ Settings in the app.
  • Enter your Portal URL, MAC address, and Serial Number (from your IPTV provider).
  • Tap Submit — your channels, movies, and series load automatically.
You need your own IPTV subscription. The app stores your details only on your device — nothing is uploaded. "Unknown sources" warnings are normal for apps installed outside the Play Store.
Android only (Fire TV, Android TV, phones & tablets). Not affiliated with any IPTV provider.
